Welcome to the GPA Calculator

Here you can input your classes and grades and we will compute your GPA for the quarter!

In the table below you may enter up to 8 courses. Please enter your class name (This is not mandatory but is here to help you organize so that you dont accidentally input the same grade for a class twice!), your current grade (A+,A,A- etc.), and the number of credit hours your course is worth. If you are putting less than 8 classes into the calculator please leave the default 0 as the credit value. If you have any questions please refer to the GPA Calculator Documentation Page.
